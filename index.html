The Programme

A clear, structured path
from uncertainty to adoption

Frontier™ unfolds in two phases - each with defined milestones, clear outputs, and built-in flexibility to match your organisation's pace and culture.

⏱ Indicative duration: 4–6 weeks  ·  Optional Pathfinder & SLT Orientation

The Pathfinder is an optional component designed for organisations that see value in first orientating their SLT and Board to the AI landscape — and to what Frontier™ can specifically offer them. It facilitates honest, open discussion about how well-placed the organisation is to leverage AI, and how transformation of this kind sits within the broader culture.

This preliminary work gives senior decision-makers the space to get comfortable with the topic, understand its key drivers, and build the confidence needed to participate fully in the transformation programme that follows.

Equally, organisations that feel ready may go directly into Phase One — and in some cases the Pathfinder may stand alone as a discrete, self-contained engagement.
